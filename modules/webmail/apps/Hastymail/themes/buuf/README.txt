buuf icon theme for Hastymail2
------------------------------

1) Unarchive into your \themes folder

2) Add the following line to your hastymail2.conf line under the THEMES heading

   theme = buuf,true,true,false

3) Recompile to an .rc and upload to your server

4) You may need to logout/login to see the theme in your list

That simple :).

Comments/Question, visit redmutt.com and comment on the blog entry "buuf theme for Hastymail2".

Icons are from mattahan (http://mattahan.deviantart.com/art/Buuf-37966044)
