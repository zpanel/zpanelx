tango icon theme for Hastymail2
-------------------------------

1) Unarchive into your \themes folder

2) Add the following line to your hastymail2.conf line under the THEMES heading

   theme = tango,true,true,false

3) Recompile to an .rc and upload to your server

4) You may need to logout/login to see the theme in your list

That simple :).

Comments/Question, visit redmutt.com and comment on the blog entry "tango theme for Hastymail2".

Icons are from the Tango Desktop Project (http://tango.freedesktop.org/Tango_Desktop_Project)
