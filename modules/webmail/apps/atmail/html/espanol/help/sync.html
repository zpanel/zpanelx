What is the $pref['brandname'] sync utility?

The utility allows you to sync your webmail account with Outlook. With it, all addressbook information you have with the webmail account are transferred over to Outlook, and vice-versa.

How do I enable Mail sync with Outlook?

To sync your Webmail account with Outlook, do the following:

  1. First, download the @Mail-sync installation utility at http://atmail.com/websync/websync-53.exe
  2. On your Microsoft Outlook window, go to Tools > Options
  1. Then, define all changes in the Mail sync utility pane. Click on Tools > Options, then go to the tab labeled "@mail Sync Configuration"


  1. Save all changes, then exit the accounts options window.

How do I sync my webmail account with Outlook?

After enabling Mail-syncing (see above), you should see a macro button labeled "@Mail sync" on your toolbar:


Click on the button, and Outlook will sync itself with your webmail account automatically.
