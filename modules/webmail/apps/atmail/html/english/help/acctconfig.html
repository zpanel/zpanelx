How can I change my outgoing email name?

Your 'Displayed Name" is the name that appears in every outgoing email message that the receiver will see as a sender. To change the "Displayed Name" you have to:

  1. Click the "Settings" from the main menu.
  2. Enter you wanted name into the "Displayed Name" field.
  3. Click Save Settings

How can I create a signature?

A signature is a message that is automatically added to the bottom of every email you send it. It can be a convenient way to put your phone number and address in the email.

  1. Click the "Settings" from the main menu.
  2. Enter your signature into the "Email Signature" field.
  3. Click Save Settings.
